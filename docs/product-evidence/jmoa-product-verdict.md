# JMOA Product Verdict

Verdict: `ONE_SERVICE_PRODUCT_WIN`.

JMOA V2 has one substantial clean no-JMOA-to-final-V2 confirmation on Doctor:
median PSS `-5,809 KB`, Private_Dirty `-5,492 KB`, and `memory.current`
`-11,845,632` bytes across three winning pairs.

PetClinic and Patient did not clear the direct screen gate. They remain useful
negative evidence and do not receive confirmation or product-win language.

The result proves that the complete system can deliver a material direct win,
but not that it does so universally. The practical product posture is
evidence-gated admission: build, materialize, screen, confirm, attribute, and
reject deployment combinations that do not improve memory.

The three-service V1-to-V2 matrix remains a successful engineering-evolution
result. It is reported separately and is not used to inflate the direct product
verdict.
