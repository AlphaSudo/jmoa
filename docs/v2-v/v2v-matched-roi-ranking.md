# V2-V Matched-Evidence ROI Ranking

ROI is recalculated only from matched bundles. The ranking combines unique
footprint, lifecycle load ratios, runtime-generated counts, histogram
persistence, cross-service/protocol coverage, safety, mutation complexity,
verification burden, and rollback burden.

Until a bundle is matched, its family remains `GENERATED_REPORT_ONLY` and is
not a prototype candidate. Spring Data remains an investigation lead, not a
preselected target.
