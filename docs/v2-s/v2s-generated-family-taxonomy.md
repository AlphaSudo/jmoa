# V2-S Generated-Family Taxonomy

V2-S maintains a stable registry in `GeneratedFamilyRegistry`. `SPRING_CGLIB`, `JDK_PROXY`, `BYTEBUDDY`, `HIBERNATE_PROXY`, `SPRING_AOT_BEAN_DEFINITIONS`, and `SPRING_AOT_REGISTRATION` are mutation-blocked by default. Lambda, Spring Data, bridge, and compiler-helper families are discovery-only until their own proof plan exists.

The registry records runtime naming patterns, semantic role, mutation risk, default admission state, and required proof before any future prototype.
