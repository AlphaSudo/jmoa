# Historical B0 vs V2 Confirmation

This document preserves the original three-pair B0-to-V2 result as historical
provenance. Its raw run-level capture directory was not retained after the
earlier release process, so it is **not** an RC2 reproducible or release-driving
claim.

The original record reported 2/3 paired wins with median PSS `-8,956 KB`,
Private_Dirty `-8,620 KB`, and `memory.current` `-11,247,616 B` under the
then-corrected protocol. The fresh RC2 exact-image five-pair replication is the
authoritative reproducible result: it is mixed (2/5 PSS wins; median PSS
`+585 KB`) and does not confirm a direct B0-to-V2 win.

See [RC2 Frozen-Image Replication](v2-rc2-frozen-image-replication.md) for the
fresh evidence and [V2 Final Memory-Win Matrix](v2-final-memory-win-matrix.md)
for the current claim boundary.
