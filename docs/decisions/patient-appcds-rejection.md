# Patient Application-CDS Rejection

Decision: dynamic Patient application CDS is blocked for the tested
single-replica deployment.

Fixed-artifact archive-economics screens and the extracted-layout common-class
study failed their frozen admission rules. No favorable archive was selected
after failure. This decision does not reject CDS generally:

- Patient stock JDK base CDS is confirmed at `-8,279 KB` median PSS;
- Patient no-CDS is independently confirmed at `-8,903 KB`;
- Doctor application CDS is confirmed for its own artifact/archive protocol.

The distinction is whether an artifact-specific Patient application archive is
mapped over the stock base archive.
